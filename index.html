Frequently Ask Questions
How do I place an order?

You may place an order through our website; by calling us at (070) 3102-4105; or by visiting our bakery.

Am I limited to the bake designs on your website?

No! Those photographs are merely examples of work we've done in the past. Please feel free to contact us over any inspirational designs you may find through other sites.

What if I don't like what I purchased?

We rarely hear complaints about our products,If you have concerns about a purchase, please kindly return the product to us on the next business day following your purchase. We do not offer refunds on returned products but may work with you on a store credit or product exchange.

What makes your products different?

Well, you might have read once or twice that we are a scratch bakery, and that fact alone sets apart from most other bakeries. This improves the taste and quality of the products we make. Our bakes are not made in factories like those you might purchase from some grocery store bakeries. We never freeze our bakes, though that too is a common practice.
